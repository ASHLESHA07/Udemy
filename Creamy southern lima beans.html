CREAMY SOUTHERN LIMA BEANS

CREAMY SOUTHERN LIMA BEANS

PREP TIME :
15 mins
COOK TIME:
2 hrs
TOTAL TIME
2 hrs 15 mins
SERVINGS
6 to 8 servings

    Ingredients ~

  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 smoked turkey wings
  • 8 cups chicken stock (preferably unsalted or low-sodium)
  • Additional salt and pepper to taste

    Pick over the dry beans:

  1. Pick over the beans, removing any dirt or debris. Rinse the beans in a colander; drain.
  2. Lima Beans in a Colander for Southern Lima Beans Recipe

    Add the onions, garlic, and seasoning:

  1. Place the beans, onion, garlic cloves, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, bay leaf, and turkey wings in a large Dutch oven or pot.
  2. Add the stock. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Once it starts to boil, decrease the heat to medium-low. Cover with a lid.
  4. Simmer for up to 2 hours or until the beans are tender to your liking. Make sure to stir the mixture a few times while cooking for a creamier sauce.

    Pick the meat from the turkey wings:

  1. Using tongs, remove the turkey wings and let them cool until you can comfortably handle them. Remove the cooked meat from the bones using a fork and shred; discard the skin. Place the meat back in the pot.
  2. Remove the bay leaf; discard.

    Season and serve:

  1. Taste the beans and season with additional salt and pepper, if needed.
  2. Serve warm.
